My Tale

Growing up during the dawn of an era of such enduring advancements like Furbies, LazerDiscs, and Windows ME, I have always had a fascination with technology. Eventually, that curiousity would lead me to the development trail I have taken. Please, take a look a look around, and let me know if my works tickle your fancy or somehow cause an insatiable appetite for candy corn.


Hailing from the urban tundra of Chicago, I am a grizzled veteran of the harsh climes of the Midwest. When I'm not developing, you can catch me seeking out local gems for food, drink, and adventure. I also try to experience adventures abroad whenever possible. Throw a novel activity my way, and I'll probably be glad to join you.
